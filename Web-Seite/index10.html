When we watch our children grow up — not in trees, not by rivers, but in the Kill Web. Trapped in a system that separates us from each other while pretending to connect us.

These books are my testimony. Not an academic work. Not a self-help book. A testimony — for those who come after us.

Der dritte Band öffnet die Frage, die die ersten beiden aufwerfen: Wie kann Zusammenarbeit zwischen Mensch und KI aussehen, ohne dass der Mensch sich selbst verliert? Nicht Technik ablehnen. The third volume opens the question raised by the first two: How can collaboration between human and AI look without the human losing themselves? Not rejecting technology. Not blindly following it.
